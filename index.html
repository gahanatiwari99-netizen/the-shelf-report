What this blog covers

The Shelf Report is an independent publication focused on the stories behind the products, deals, and decisions that shape everyday life — from the geopolitics of a supply chain to the lawsuit that quietly reformulated your favourite moisturiser.

I write at the intersections most business coverage misses: where FMCG meets foreign policy, where a skincare ingredient's chemistry collides with a courtroom, where India's growth story plays out in the fast-moving consumer space.
